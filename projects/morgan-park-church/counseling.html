Counseling

Professional counseling is offered as an outreach dimension of the Morgan Park Baptist Church Center for Spiritual Health to individuals and families in our community.

Judy Healy, B.A., M.S.W., L.C.S.W. (Director of Counseling Services)

Judy has a B.A. from National Lewis University, and a M.S.W. from Loyola University of Chicago. She has experience counseling individuals, couples and families, as well as 25 years of experience as a school social worker. She provided individual, and group counseling to students, and consultation to parents/guardians and teachers. She also has specialized training and experience in crisis intervention, and currently serves on a response team that provides support to emergency services personnel, search and rescue personnel, and first responders during critical incidents.

The Center for Spiritual Health at Morgan Park Baptist Church

The Center for Spiritual Health is a Community Outreach of Morgan Park Baptist Church. The Center has been established to bring opportunities for spiritual growth, health and healing to the people of Beverly/Morgan Park. The members of Morgan Park Baptist Church recognize that there are four dimensions to true wholeness: spiritual health, psychological health, physical health and social health. All four dimensions must be addressed if one is to be truly healthy. We believe health, healing and wholeness are vital dimensions of our church’s vision and mission. We affirm a benevolent, loving, compassionate God while embracing a healing and healthy Christianity.

The Center for Spiritual Health offers a variety of services and programs designed to help bring healing, wholeness, and holiness to the people of our church and our community. PLEASE SEE CHURCH CALENDAR.

The Director of the CSH, Judy Healy, has a B.A. from National Lewis University, and a M.S.W. from Loyola University of Chicago. She has experience counseling individuals, couples and families, as well as 25 years of experience as a school social worker. She provided individual, and group counseling to students, and consultation to parents/guardians and teachers. She also has specialized training and experience in crisis intervention, and currently serves on a response team that provides support to emergency services personnel, search and rescue personnel, and first responders during critical incidents.

Professional Counseling - As a vital dimension of The Center for Spiritual Health, we provide professional counseling to those individuals and families in our community who are in need of counseling.
