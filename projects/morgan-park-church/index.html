A Cordial Invitation from Morgan Park Baptist Church

Morgan Park Baptist Church has been serving the Morgan Park / Beverly Hills neighborhood of Chicago for over 135 years. We extend this invitation to our neighbors to come worship and share fellowship with us. This invitation is especially for those who do not have a church home and those who are new to our neighborhood.

11:00 AM - Worship Service in our parlor. First Sundays are family worship with Holy Communion. Worship service is lived-streamed on Morgan Park Baptist Church Facebook page at 11 AM.
